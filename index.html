Mondstadt

Mondstadt is one of the seven nations in Teyvat, and the first in which the Traveler starts to look for their lost sibling. It is the city-state that worships Barbatos, the Anemo Archon.

Liyue

Liyue is one of the seven nations in Teyvat. It is the city-state that worships Morax, the Geo Archon.

Inazuma

Inazuma is one of the seven nations in Teyvat. It is the city-state that worships the Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon, who is also the leader of its governing body, the Inazuma Shogunate.

Elements

The Main Elements of Nature

Pyro Element

Applies the Pyro status. (Makes the character take damage over time)

Hydro Element

Applies the Wet status. (Makes character vulnerable to damage from nearby Electro-Charged enemies)

Electro Element

Applies the Electro status. (Causes character movement and attacks to stop momentarily)

Cryo Element

Applies the Cryo status. (Reduces Movement Speed and Attack Speed)
